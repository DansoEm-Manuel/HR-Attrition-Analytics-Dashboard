# 📖 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most critical challenges affecting organizational productivity, cost efficiency, and workforce stability.

This project presents an **end-to-end HR Analytics solution** that analyzes employee attrition patterns using a combination of **Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI**. The goal is to transform raw HR data into actionable insights that help organizations understand:

* Who is leaving the organization
* Why employees are leaving
* Which employees are at highest risk of attrition

The final output is an interactive **HR Attrition Dashboard** designed for HR decision-makers and business leaders.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall employee attrition trends
* Identify high-risk departments and job roles
* Understand demographic patterns of attrition
* Investigate key drivers of employee turnover
* Build an interactive HR analytics dashboard
* Develop employee-level risk insights using drill-through
* Provide data-driven HR retention recommendations

---

# 🛠️ Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel → Data cleaning & preprocessing
* MySQL (SQL) → Data validation & exploration
* Python (Pandas, NumPy) → Data transformation & feature engineering
* Power BI → Dashboard development & visualization

# 📂 Project Scope

# 🔍 Key Insights

* Overall attrition rate: **16.1%**
* Highest attrition in:

  * Research & Development
  * Sales Department
* Most affected roles:

  * Laboratory Technicians
  * Sales Executives
* Highest risk group:

  * Employees aged **25–34**
  * Employees with **0–2 years tenure**
* Key drivers of attrition:

  * Low job satisfaction
  * Poor work-life balance
  * Excessive overtime
  * Low income levels
